Artist Biography

Meet the Artist

Jonas Gayer is a painter working in acrylics and oils. He specializes in collages, abstract art, and figurative paintings. Jonas' work has been exhibited in New York City, the Hamptons, and on Shelter Island. His studio is located on Shelter Island where he has been a resident for over fifty years.


His work ranges from scenic views to portraits, to cubism, to his unique style. His most recent work is a combination of dynamic colors and textures which take on a life of their own. These paintings encourage the viewer to be creative and interpret the meaning behind the artwork. Jonas' work has been influenced by the exquisite views of Shelter Island, his family, and friends.
